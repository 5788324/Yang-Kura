## Focus

MVP-45 continues media-product polish on the home page, recent playback, and continue-listening surfaces.

- Promote continue-listening and recent playback above engineering/status information.
- Keep quick entries for 音声库、音乐库、歌单、导入 / 更新资源库.

- SQLite
- downloader

- absolutePath or file:// exposure to renderer
- new backend scanning, writing, playback, or lyrics capability
